About Me

Hi! I'm Andrey. This website was made to showcase my experiences and talents, as well as my passions. I hope you enjoy reading about me.

I'm currently a Junior at Carnegie Mellon University pursuing a B.S. in Chemical Engineering as well as a minor in Computer Science. I'm passionate about using computing power to solve problems in the Chemical Engineering world, whether it be through optimizing processes, modeling, or using data analysis to create more reliable and accurate results. I don't have much experience in these fields, but I have developed a strong base of knowledge to begin to do so in the coming years. Most recently, I was a QA lab tech in the Chemistry lab of PBM Nutritionals LLC, a pharmaceutical company. Besides work, I have taken part in a number of coding projects including working on expanding the code of Pyomo, a process optimization library. On campus, I have a presence on the Mr. Yuk Ultimate team and the Delta Tau Delta Fraternity.

Off campus and out of work, I run, hike, bike, and ski. I've lived in Vermont for a long time and I enjoy the privilege of the outdoors.

Portfolio

Pyomo Optimization Library

I worked to expand the Pyomo library by implementing McCormick envelopes. A McCormick envelope is a transformation of bilinear optimization problems used to relax them into convex problems, which are much easier to solve and require less computational resources to reach comparable results.

15-112 Term Project: Rock Climber

This was a term project in which I created a video game using python's tkinter library for animation. The game also implemented several storage and display optimizations to ensure that it ran efficiently throughout the entire play period.

Bluetooth vehicle using PS2 Conroller
